QUICK FACTS

Habitat: Mana-rich environments, such as wizarding structures or magical biomes.

Communication: Incessant buzzing.

Tactics: Instinctual creatures that primarily seek mana. They are sometimes weaponized against arcane foes in "Buzz Bombs".

Indicators: Rusty imbued items, a single Bizzer moving along a wall, that horrible buzzing noise.

YIELD

Arcane Powder: 1 Unit of Arcane Powder.

Buzz Bombs: 2 Buzz Bombs (Thrown Weapon range, 1 Heart swarm) can be created if the swarm is captured unharmed in a glass jar.
